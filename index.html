Data Preparation, Feature Engineering, & Algorithm Implementation

Data is the lifeblood that drives any successful AI & ML business strategy. We begin to develop this strategy for clients by closely assisting with data gathering, cleaning, and preparation (data labeling). Once data is available in data lakes or data warehouses, we then work side by side with the client and domain experts to develop a deeper and understanding of the data. Following this rigorous process allow us to understand and extract key features of the data, a process more formally known as Feature Engineering. Once completed, MavenCode assists with choosing the right AI or ML algorithm for the data, and helps guide the client through the entire training, testing and validation process. Finally, our consultants work with client developers and data scientists to put the AI & ML model into production so it can be used for prediction and support business operations.

Data Pipelines

Big Data Requires Big Infrastructure. At MavenCode, we build scalable AI & ML data pipelines for organizations interested in monetizing the ever increasing amounts of data generated by their customers, products (e.g. IoT devices), or services. These pipelines easily adapt to data of any frequency and can scale up and down easily as the velocity of data increases or decreases. Engineered to be robust and portable end to end solutions, MavenCode’s data pipelines enable large scale AI & ML modeling for data scientists and data engineers in an organization, helping to cut down the time needed to productionize a model, and therefore increasing overall data strategy ROI.

Oil and Gas

Machine learning (ML) is transforming the ways in which oil and gas exploration is done. With ML techniques, researchers, geoscientists and reservoir engineers can leverage insights from advanced deep learning models to identify complex patterns such as faults or stratigraphic geometries from seismic datasets in a sophisticated ML-based workflow for multiple iterations and therefore accelerating the whole process. By leveraging on Google Cloud technology our team is actively working on data-pipelines optimized to orchestrate this kind of high capacity workflow that enhances collaboration between data scientist, geoscientist and every stakeholder in the organization.

Healthcare

The growing abundance of healthcare data today comes from a variety of different sources and forms, such as Electronic Health Records (EHRs), genomic sequencing, medical IoT devices, and many more. This makes the healthcare industry a very interesting one to apply Machine Learning, and the diversity of data formats and contexts makes it a big challenge to solve Healthcare Analytics problems using conventional means. At MavenCode we thrive in solving complex problems of this nature by working with medical experts in this field to craft out a clear and well-defined approach to harness ML workflow techniques. This helps us integrate and connect all these disparate systems and derive intelligent insights to better improve patient treatment, while at the same time reducing cost. We leverage Google Cloud technology products like BigQuery, Pubsub and Cloud ML in our solutions to rapidly prototype and expand concepts into deployable production implementations that help augment the doctor's insight and knowledge about their patients.

Retail and E-commerce

Every business wants to achieve growth at great scale and better serve its customers to build up their brand equity and achieve customer loyalty for life. The challenge of doing this at scale becomes enormous without the power of ML techniques to understand each customer uniquely, for instance: their preferences, historical buying trends, and taste. Building the infrastructure to handle this kind of data capacity at scale and from scratch is not a trivial task. However, by leveraging Google Cloud technology tools like Google Managed Kubernetes, Dataflow, Pubsub, Storage and Cloud ML, we successfully help our customers go from “zero to hero” in a matter of days. With Google Managed services, we find it easy to quickly deploy Proof of Concept (PoC) implementations that we gradually mature into a production grade quality, without having to worry too much about the underlying infrastructure or how it might scale.

Finance

At its core the financial industry has always been driven by data in the form of transactions - ranging from a simple exchange of money or credit in a store for goods, all the way to multi-million dollar automated stock trades that execute in mere fractions of a second. Given that banking institutions are in the business of both warehousing (holding) and trading risk, the opportunity for advanced data analytics to gain a competitive advantage in the financial industry is enormous. For instance, AI & ML modeling can be used to more accurately assess the default probabilities on a portfolio of loans, predict whether or not a given credit card transaction is fraudulent, or even predict the price of securities themselves (stocks, bonds, futures, etc.) leading to new automated trading strategies. However, all this modeling requires enormous data and data infrastructure to be done successfully. At MavenCode, we leverage Google Cloud technology products like BigQuery, Cloud Bigtable, Pubsub, and Cloud ML to build scalable, end to end data pipelines that can be easily tuned to ingest anything from large batch jobs to high-frequency market data. The data pipelines enable us to work together with our clients to develop advanced analytical solutions that provide them with an edge in the hyper-competitive financial market place.
